AVATAR

 

WHY?

  • Enhancing the level of self-awareness.

    It easily happens that people think they worked hard all day long, but not much in effect. If they are aware of how they spend a day objectively, it is possible to make a wiser time-management strategy by removing unnecessary choices. Not working-hard-mode just to return to the same state, but to evaluate objectively to make true improvements. If you cannot measure, you cannot control it. A leading clinical psychologist Jordan Peterson emphasized on individual responsibility as the first step for solving big problems facing humanity like global warming.

  • To make deeper relations

    The complexity of social media networking is soaring. People long for likes and attention from other people by faking and manipulating reality, which result in the arrogant vanity and depression. Instead of adjusting to and defining oneself by external stimulus, forming deeper humane relationships by showing share one's normal life.

    •  

    What is it?

    A self-oriented virtual platform service that a user can log everyday life to know thyself and offer others(friends, acquaintance, potential friends, etc) actual timeline-based accumulated data as factual evidence to let them better understand of you for the purpose of building stronger and meaningful relations; compared to the other outdated social medias that is dragging people into a quagmire of self-hate.

     

    The Vision

  • To increase the level of self-awareness(meta-cognition) of you
  • To reduce small talks like 'How are you?' and 'what are you up to?'. To make the conversation go further and fruitful. Your people can understand what you have been doing with a wide spectrum of proved data.
  • To gain factual evidence that can prove others what you do and how you do (eg. My hobby is reading books).
  • To make you feel less lonely(avatar, deeper relations)
  • Utilize your avatar in other upcoming future metaverse platforms or services.
  • Each person get a service like Jarvis from the 'Iron man'
